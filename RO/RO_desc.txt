Fjør Tal is a roleplaying overhaul which adds a number of roleplaying and simulation features as well as other changes for more varied, challenging, and realistic gameplay.

Compatible with Skyrim Anniversary Edition Content, including Survival Mode.

Requires Skyrim Special Edition v


*** Features ***

Damaged Weapons and Armor:
Weapons and armor can become damaged and can be repaired at a forge if you have the required smithing perk. Only basic and crafted weapons and armor can be damaged. A weapon may be damaged when striking or parrying an attack while taking a hit can damage armor.

Last Stand:
When health drops below 25% time slows and damage to the player is reduced in order to enable a Last Stand, extending the most intense action-packed moments of gameplay.

Curse of the Dead:
Taking gold and other treasure from crypts may cause the player to incur the "Curse of the Dead". When cursed the player does half damage to undead.

Drunkenness:
New visual effects from drinking alcohol.

Carry Weight Settings Menu:
Adjust your maximum Carry Weight

Immersion (Roleplaying Bonus):
New player stat that is gained through performing roleplaying actions. When full, the player is granted a bonus to XP earning rate. Immersion resets when the player sleeps.

Sit and Talk:
Sit when talking to characters to have better chances to succeed with persuasion attempts, better prices with vendors, and earn extra Speechcraft XP from conversations.

Unencumbered Rest:
Rest while carrying less weight to wake with a higher Immersion value.

Daily Immersion Action:
Doing any one of the following actions grants a bonus to Immersion until the player sleeps.
- Eating a food item
- Sitting for an hour or more (Game Time)
- Working at a crafting station for an hour or more (Game Time)


*** Gameplay Adjustments ***

Reduced TimeScale: 20 >> 10 (Game time progress at half speed)
No Starting Spells (Find Healing in Introduction Quest)


*** New Items ***

Silver Arrows
Damaged versions of basic weapons and armor
Broken pieces of some weapon types


*** Animal Loot Overhaul ***

Animals, such as dear, no longer have treasure in their loot, but now have more meat and their full set of useful body parts.
Meat items and cooking recipes have been added for wolves, saber cats, bears, and more.


*** Enemy Poisons Overhaul ***

Enemy poison durations are greatly increased, but damaged is decreased.
Additional paralysis effect is added to some poison attacks.
Additional visual effects are added to some poison effects.


*** Enemy spells and inventory ***

Bandit equipment remains low level (Fur, Iron, Steel)
High level bandits use tempered versions of basic equipment rather than more valuable equipment.
Draugr bosses have new summoning abilities.
Silver Hand Weapons include damaged and tempered silver weapons
